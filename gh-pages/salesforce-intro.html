Salesforce : A simple Introduction

Salesforce.  You must have heard that name before, that’s why you are here. And most probably the other guides and Salesforce explainers are more focused on selling you the product salesforce, rather than helping you understand what salesforce is.

In brief : Salesforce is a platform (infrastructure + service - software and support). Which is cloud (internet app) based. Which runs custom salesforce software. Which is configurable and customizable to incorporate almost any managed process.

That’s the basics of Salesforce.But how does it help you? What makes it so powerful, that almost all businesses want to adopt it.

Businesses operations are divided into categories. i.e.  Sales. Marketing. Service.

And businesses (processes) can be industry or domain specific i.e. Finance. Healthcare. Telecom. Government. Non-profit.

Customization is the key here. Different operations or Industry domains have different processes. Salesforce has built a specific suite of apps/tools around them and packaged them into a product like sales cloud, service cloud, commerce cloud and so on..Salesforce has industry specific tools built on their platform i.e vlocity for telecommunications, veeva for healthcare and many others.

Business processes which run on salesforce are backed up by their solutions(based on AI, Data Visualization Chatbots, Machine Learning). Salesforce keeps upgrading its feature suites every season (winter, summer, spring).
